How to install Apio General drivers for windows:
———————————————————————————————————————————————

If your computer have not Digital Signature OFF you must disable this check. In your prompt paste “Bcdedit.exe /set nointegritychecks ON” 

1. Insert your General on a free USB port on your computer
2. Go to Control Panel then click on System and Security
3. Click on System and open the Device Manager
4. Under Ports(COM & LPT), you should see a port called Apio.
5. Right click on it, and click su “Update Driver Software”.
6. Now choose, “Browse my computer for Driver software”.
7. Select the “apio.inf”. Wait meanwhile Windows will finish up the driver installation.

Now you are on your way!
